Story
I envisioned this place to be a forum, where foodies can share their passion.

We are building a communy where food lovers can post a picture of their dinner plate, and see what others eat... around the same time, or somewhere nereby.

That's the key: time-wise and location-wise nearby. You can browse the feed, or browse the map. Or (soon enough) do both at the same time!

Go out for dinner, and take a pic of your order. Or check out other's recent posts to get a tip for today's dinner location. You can discvover new restaurats this way.

Try out your grandma's recipe at home, and share your moment of pride with the world.
